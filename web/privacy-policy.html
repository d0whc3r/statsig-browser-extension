Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 31, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how the Statsig Browser Extension handles information when you use the extension.

Information we process

  • Extension settings you provide, such as API-related configuration values.
  • Statsig evaluation data displayed in the extension interface.
  • Temporary runtime information required to show gates, experiments, and configs.

How information is used

  • To display Statsig entities and their current evaluation state.
  • To allow authorized override actions initiated by you.
  • To support debugging and QA workflows.

Data sharing

The extension does not sell personal data. Data is only sent to services required for Statsig functionality and only as needed to process requests you trigger.

Security

We use reasonable measures to protect data handled by the extension. You are responsible for safeguarding your own credentials and access keys.

Your choices

You can remove extension data by clearing browser extension storage or uninstalling the extension.
